## Missing High-Value SEO Questions

Based on research of common patient queries and search intent, the following questions are **frequently searched** but **missing**:

### Critical Missing Questions

1. **"Does acupuncture hurt?"** / **"Are acupuncture needles painful?"**
   - **Search Volume**: Very High
   - **Why Critical**: #1 barrier to first-time patients
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Not usually. Acupuncture needles are hair-thin and much finer than injection needles. You may feel a tiny pinch during insertion, followed by sensations like warmth, tingling, or a dull ache (called 'De Qi'), which indicates the treatment is working. Most patients find it relaxing."

2. **"Is acupuncture covered by insurance?"** / **"Does insurance cover acupuncture in BC?"**
   - **Search Volume**: Very High  
   - **Why Critical**: Major decision factor for booking
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Yes, most Extended Health Plans in British Columbia cover acupuncture treatments by a Registered Acupuncturist (R.Ac.). We provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your insurance for reimbursement. Check your specific plan for coverage limits."

3. **"What can acupuncture treat?"** / **"What conditions does acupuncture help?"**
   - **Search Volume**: High
   - **Why Critical**: Educates potential patients on applicability
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Acupuncture is recognized by the WHO for treating chronic pain, headaches, stress, anxiety, digestive issues (IBS, bloating), insomnia, menstrual disorders, infertility support, and musculoskeletal conditions. We create personalized treatment plans for your specific needs."

4. **"How long does an acupuncture session last?"**
   - **Search Volume**: High
   - **Why Critical**: Time commitment concern
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Your initial visit lasts 90 minutes, including consultation and treatment. Follow-up sessions are 60 minutes. Needles typically remain in place for 20-30 minutes while you relax."

5. **"When will I see results from acupuncture?"**
   - **Search Volume**: High
   - **Why Critical**: Expectation management
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Many patients notice improvements after 1-3 sessions for acute conditions. Chronic issues typically show meaningful progress within 6-8 treatments. Consistency is key for lasting results."

6. **"Is acupuncture safe?"** / **"What are the risks?"**
   - **Search Volume**: Medium-High
   - **Why Critical**: Safety/trust building
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Yes, acupuncture is extremely safe when performed by a licensed practitioner. We use single-use, sterile needles and follow strict safety protocols. Serious side effects are very rare. Minor bruising or temporary soreness may occur."

7. **"What should I wear to acupuncture?"**
   - **Search Volume**: Medium
   - **Why Critical**: Reduces first-time anxiety
   - **SEO Value**: ⭐⭐⭐
   - **Recommended Answer**: "Wear loose, comfortable clothing that can be easily rolled up to access points on your arms, legs, and back. We can provide a gown if needed for treatments on your torso."

## SEO Best Practices for FAQs

### 1. **Question Format**
- Use natural language questions that match voice search
- Include location when relevant: "Does insurance cover acupuncture **in BC**?"
- Match how people actually search

### 2. **Answer Format**  
- Keep answers concise (50-150 words ideal for Featured Snippets)
- Front-load the answer (most important info first)
- Use simple, accessible language
- Include specific details (times, prices, processes)

### 3. **Schema Markup Priority**
When implementing FAQ schema:
- **Homepage**: Focus on conversion-driving questions
- **Treatments page**: Focus on educational/trust-building questions
- **Individual treatment pages**: Include treatment-specific questions already present

### 4. **Strategic Keywords**
Ensure FAQs capture these high-value search terms:
- "acupuncture new westminster"
- "does acupuncture hurt"
- "acupuncture insurance bc"
- "how many acupuncture sessions"
- "what does acupuncture treat"
- "tcm clinic near me"
