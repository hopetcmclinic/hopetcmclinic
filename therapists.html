Embracing Holistic Healing

"My practice reflects the Taoist principle of harmony between human and nature."

Dr. Eva is a licensed acupuncturist and Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) registered with the CTCMA of British Columbia. She graduated in 2019 from the esteemed Tzu Chi International College of Traditional Chinese Medicine in Vancouver.

Specializations

With a deep passion for holistic health, Dr. Eva focuses on treating the root causes of:

Digestive Issues
Emotional Imbalances
Women’s Health
Post-COVID Symptoms
Sports Injuries
Acute & Chronic Pain

Integrative Approach

Utilizing a powerful combination of modalities to achieve balance:

Acupuncture Herbal Medicine Cupping Moxibustion Gua Sha Ear Seeds

Philosophy

A strong advocate for alternative medicine, Dr. Eva believes in reducing reliance on pharmaceuticals when natural solutions can support long-term wellness.

Inspired by Taoism, she encourages clients to embrace a balanced lifestyle where physical health, emotional well-being, and spiritual harmony thrive together.
