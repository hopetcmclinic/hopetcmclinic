(Eva) Fang Yuan - Registered Acupuncturist, Doctor of TCM

Dr. Eva is a licensed acupuncturist and Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) registered with the CTCMA of British Columbia. She graduated in 2019 from the esteemed Tzu Chi International College of Traditional Chinese Medicine in Vancouver.


With a deep passion for holistic health, Dr. Eva specializes in treating a variety of conditions, including digestive issues, emotional imbalances, women’s health issues, and post-COVID-19 symptoms such as shortness of breath and racing heart rate. She is also highly skilled in addressing acute and chronic pain, particularly related to sports injuries.


Dr. Eva takes an integrative approach to healing, utilizing a combination of Acupuncture, Herbal, Cupping, Moxibustion, Scraping, and Ear Seeds. By blending these powerful modalities, she helps clients achieve balance, promote natural healing, and improve overall well-being.


A strong advocate for alternative medicine, Dr. Eva believes in the importance of reducing reliance on pharmaceuticals and invasive surgeries when possible. Her goal is to offer clients safe, natural solutions that support long-term health and wellness.


Inspired by Taoism, Dr. Eva’s practice reflects the Taoist principle of "harmony between human and nature." She encourages clients to embrace a balanced and natural lifestyle, where physical health, emotional well-being, and spiritual harmony all thrive.


Outside of her practice, Dr. Eva enjoys spending quality time with family and friends, often sharing homemade treats and the joys of nourishing connection.
