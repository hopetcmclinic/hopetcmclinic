<h3 class="subtitle text-2xl font-serif text-primary mb-4">Nature's Pharmacy</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
Herbal medicine is a cornerstone of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), harnessing the therapeutic properties of plants and minerals to address a wide range of health conditions. Unlike western pharmaceuticals which often target specific symptoms, herbal formulations are meticulously crafted to treat the underlying root cause of your condition.
</p>

<p class="mb-6">

A formula might contain anywhere from 5 to 15 different herbs, working synergistically to restore balance to your body.
</p>
